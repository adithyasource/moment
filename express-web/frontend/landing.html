moment is a minimalist micro blogging platform built for the terminal

create an account and start posting to your own lightweight .txt url within seconds!

most personal blogs are filled with gibberish and face their timely death when their user doesn’t care enough for it. the terminal interface that moment provides you encourages you to cut the crap and helps you get your thoughts out to the internet.
